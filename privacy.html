03 // privacy

your data is yours.

privacy first philosophy

at **infiniware**, we believe privacy isn't just a feature—it's a fundamental human right. **we will never sell your personal data.** our philosophy is simple: we don't want what we don't need. your data isn't an asset to us; your trust is the only thing that matters.

1. what we collect

- **authentication data**: for signups, we store only what's necessary (email/hashed password). - **voluntary content**: posts you share in the community hub.

2. what we do NOT collect

- we do not use third-party trackers or ads. - **we will never sell, lease, or share your data.** - your location for al-muslim stays strictly on your device.

3. technical security

we use industry-standard encryption for data transit (tls) and at rest. security is built into the architecture from the first line of code, prioritizing isolation and minimal access privileges.

account deletion

you have full control. you can request a complete data purge or account deletion at any time through your dashboard or by contacting support.
