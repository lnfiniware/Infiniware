privacy first, always

i want to be very clear: we will never sell your personal data. privacy isn't a secondary goal for me; it's the foundation of everything. we believe privacy matters more than anything else in software design. al-muslim geolocation will always be local, and your community posts are yours. trust is our only asset.

why everything is lowercase

i get asked this a lot. lowercase just feels quieter. more structural. no screaming headers, no corporate enthusiasm. just the code and the content. the red "I" is the only thing that gets a capital—it's the anchor.
