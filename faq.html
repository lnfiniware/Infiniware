frequently asked questions

what defines the "infiniware" ecosystem?

infiniware is a focused hub hosting developer-centric products, technical logs (blog), and a community space. it is designed as a calm, structured alternative to traditional, noisy software platforms.

why is the entire site lowercase?

typography is a structural tool for us. lowercase represents a quiet, non-aggressive presence. the only exception is the red "I" in our logo, which serves as a single point of engineered emphasis.

is al-muslim free and open?

yes. al-muslim is a service for the community, built with a strict privacy-first approach. while the core application is free, we maintain high standards of development and accuracy through the support of the infiniware community.

how are prayer times and locations handled?

we prioritize local-first computation. your location is used directly on your device using standard astronomical formulas to calculate prayer schedules. your coordinates never leave your hardware.

can i contribute to the blog or community?

absolutely. you can authenticate via github to participate in the community feed. for technical contributions or guest blog posts, please contact our support team.
